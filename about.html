ABOUT ME


Hello, my name is Joshua Sharp.


I am currently a Junior in the Leeds School of Business at the University of Colorado Boulder. I am studying Entrepreneurship and Marketing with a Minor in Creative Technology and Design.


I spent most of my time outside of pursuing my education on directing CU's Esports Program. As the Director of Esports at CU Boulder, I spend a lot of time doing administrative work by overseeing all of the organization's team managers and coaches. I also spend time coaching the Valorant Team.


Before becoming the Director of Esports, I actively competed on the Rocket League and Counter-Strike Teams. Now, in my Junior Year, I am actively pursuing competitive Apex Legends.


EXPERIENCE




As the Director of Esports, I have learned a lot about working with and managing people of all different types of personalities. I spend most of my working with player talent directly and carrying out Administrative, Marketing and Media work within the organization. Along with this I spend my weekends setting up esports competition rooms and running events such as intramurals and esports tournaments. In the future, I am looking to work as a General Manager or Team Manager at a professional esports organization.





As an esports coach, I have experience coaching many titles including Rocket League, Valorant, Apex Legends, and Counter-Strike. I transitioned into the coaching role after being a competitor in all four of these titles. As a coach I focus mostly on player communication and managing player physical and mental health.
