    Challenge 5: Accessibility Statement

    From looking at the HTML Challenges, I came to the realization that there are numerous accessibility issues including poorly located and spaced buttons, a non-functioning chess board and no text to speech integration. The groups most affected by these accessibility challenges would include general users, users looking to play chess and vision impaired users.

    When looking at potential solutions, I would look to clean up the formatting and stylization of the buttons so they stand out more and are located in plain sight. I would look to make the chess board functional by adding the ability to move one piece at a time. Lastly, I would integrate a keyboard shortcut that reads the text out loud as well as the location of the chess pieces.
